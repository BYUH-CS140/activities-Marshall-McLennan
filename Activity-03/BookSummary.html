Origin of Manga

Manga is a Japanese word that means "whimsical pictures." It is a style of comic books and graphic novels that originated in Japan. Manga has a long history, dating back to the 12th century, but it became popular in its modern form in the late 19th century.

Today, manga is enjoyed by people of all ages and is published in various genres, including action, romance, horror, and fantasy. It has also gained international popularity, with many manga series being translated into different languages.

Most Popular Manga

  1. Attack on Titan
    • Genre: Action, Horror, Mystery
    • Author: Hajime Isayama
    • Summary: In a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to giant humanoid creatures known as Titans, a group of young soldiers fights to protect their city and uncover the truth behind the Titans.
  2. One Piece
    • Genre: Adventure, Fantasy
    • Author: Eiichiro Oda
    • Summary: Follow Monkey D. Luffy and his crew of pirates as they search for the ultimate treasure known as One Piece, while facing powerful enemies and forming strong bonds along the way.
  3. Naruto
    • Genre: Action, Adventure, Fantasy
    • Author: Masashi Kishimoto
    • Summary: Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with dreams of becoming the strongest ninja and leader of his village, embarks on a journey filled with challenges, friendships, and self-discovery.
