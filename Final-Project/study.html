Study Tips

  • Use the Pomodoro technique: study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break.
  • Summarize what you just read or watched in your own words.
  • Switch up your study locations to stay mentally fresh.
  • Use visual aids like charts, flashcards, or concept maps.
  • Practice active recall and self-quizzing.
  • Teach the concept to someone else to deepen your understanding.
  • Make sure your space is clean, well-lit, and free from distractions.
  • Keep water nearby and take regular stretch breaks.
