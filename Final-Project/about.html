About Faithful Focus

Faithful Focus is a cozy scripture study website designed to promote peace, positivity, and focus. It offers a warm, calming interface where users can enjoy scripture study with ambient music and beautiful visuals.

Primary Purpose

The goal is to provide a place where people can create a peaceful atmosphere for studying, reflecting, and resting. It supports mental health and emotional well-being by encouraging positive, uplifting spiritual engagement.

Target Audience

  • College students (ages 18–26)
  • Students looking for a place to enjoy scriptures and study

Key Features

🎵 Music Player

  • Play/Pause music
  • Choose from peaceful playlists
  • Adjust volume

📓 Journal

A space to write down reflections, thoughts, and inspiration.

🎯 Focus Mode

Minimal interface that hides UI, showing only the scripture and music.

🖼️ Cozy Visuals

  • Forest desk view
  • Beach desk view
  • Busy city desk view

📖 Scripture Rotator

  • Displays a new scripture every 30–60 seconds
  • Categories: “Peace”, “Love”, “Strength”, “Wisdom”
  • Fade-in/out animations
